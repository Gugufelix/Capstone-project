The best carrot cake

Recipe


Indulge on my most favourite carrot cake recipe. It is simple , sweet , flavourful and most of all moist.

About the cake

This carrot cake sets the standard for carrot cakes everywhere. Most of its flavour comes from brown sugar , ginger ,cinammon , ginger nutmeg and obviouly carrots.Ginger adds the most delicious zing but it is not overempowering at all. The cake is dense , but each forkful tastes super soft and extra lush. If made ahead , the flavour intensifies and the cream cheeses frosting seeps into the layers , creating an even more tender bite.
